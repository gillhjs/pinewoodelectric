FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our electrical and automation services.

General

About Our Services

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Pinewood Electrical and Automation Ltd. is fully licensed and insured to operate in British Columbia. All work is performed to BC Electrical Code standards.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Surrey, Langley, Delta, Burnaby, Vancouver, White Rock, and the broader Lower Mainland region.

Do you handle both residential and commercial projects?

Yes. We work across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors — from home panel upgrades and EV charger installations to full industrial automation and control panel design.

How do I get a quote?

Contact us via our contact form, call us at (778) 387-0031, or email pinewoodelectric@hotmail.com. We typically respond within 24–48 hours.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes. Emergency electrical service is available on request. Contact us directly by phone for urgent situations.

Pricing & Process

Pricing & How We Work

Are your prices transparent?

Yes. We provide honest, upfront quotes with no hidden fees. You'll know the scope and cost before any work begins.

Do you pull permits?

Yes. All work requiring permits is properly permitted and inspected. This protects your home, your insurance, and ensures code compliance.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the scope. A panel upgrade or EV charger installation is typically completed in one day. Larger commercial or automation projects are scoped and scheduled individually.

Do you clean up after the job?

Absolutely. We follow a strict "leave-it-clean" policy — every project is finished the way we'd want it done in our own home.

Automation & Industrial

Automation Services

What automation services do you offer?

We offer PLC programming, HMI development, control panel design and fabrication, motor controls, VFD installation, and full system integration and commissioning.

Can you work with our existing equipment?

Yes. We assess your existing systems first and design solutions that integrate with your current infrastructure wherever possible, minimizing replacement costs.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes. We offer preventive maintenance, system diagnostics, and ongoing optimization support to keep your systems running reliably.
